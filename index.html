GroundTruth is a peer-attestation platform that helps Ghanaian landowners record their plot boundaries, get verified by the people who know the land — neighbours, family elders, community surveyors — and carry the trust with them wherever their plot is discussed.

We don't decide ownership. Courts and the Lands Commission do. What we do is surface the community signals that today only exist in WhatsApp threads, neighbourhood meetings, and the memory of elders — and make them portable.
